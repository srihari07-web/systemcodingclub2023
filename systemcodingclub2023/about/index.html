Embedded C

Embedded C is a programming language used for programming microcontrollers and other small devices. The embedded C community is made up of programmers, engineers, and hobbyists who use and develop software for embedded systems. They often share knowledge and resources, such as tutorials, sample code, and libraries, to help others learn and work with embedded C. Additionally, there are various online forums, discussion groups, and other platforms where members of the embedded C community can connect and collaborate.

Matlab

MATLAB is a numerical computing environment and programming language. The MATLAB community is made up of researchers, engineers, scientists, and students who use and develop software with MATLAB. They often share knowledge and resources, such as tutorials, sample code, and toolboxes, to help others learn and work with MATLAB. Additionally, there are various online forums, discussion groups, and other platforms where members of the MATLAB community can connect and collaborate.
