About

Career guidance helps learners understand their strengths and weaknesses and then match them with their skills and interests so that they can get the best suitable career choice. The main purpose of career guidance is for learners to make better SYSTEM CODING CLUB , and occupational choices. Our motive of this club is to help students with guidance of both academics and as well as career, this club comprises four groups each represents specific domain like verilog, Matlab, Embedded C and Coding CULTURE. We give students the necessary help by providing them practice problems, study material and clarifying their doubts.
